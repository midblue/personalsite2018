About Me

I'm Jasper, a UX Engineer and Designer based on the U.S. West coast.

As a designer, I work across the spectrum of UX, interactive, and visual design with companies like Adaptive Path, Blue Rocket and Beyond UX. I speak at and facilitate design events, such as the 2017 Tokyo Global Goals Jam. I mentor up-and-coming designers, as well as post about design topics like the intersection of games and UX.

As a developer, I design, build, and test full-stack experiences using tech like Vue, React, Express, and Firebase. While I lived in Tokyo, I built live prototypes of new technologies with the research team at Cygames. I'm a tinkerer by nature, as evidenced by my Github page full of tests and projects.

Personally, I've spent the last few years in Tokyo, Japan and I speak Japanese fluently. I'm a huge traveler – from 2013 to 2017 I moved to seven new places, all carrying one backpack. I'm also an esports lover: Super Smash Bros. Melee, League of Legends, Starcraft (Brood War and 2), Overwatch, speedruns – I watch it all.

If you're interested in working together, or if you just want to know what I'm up to, dont hesitate to email me or check out my LinkedIn page to stay up to date.
